About Bananacks

Built on India's
Most Fertile Ground

Bananacks Pvt Ltd is a fresh produce export company founded in Jalgaon, Maharashtra — the district that produces over 30% of India's total banana output. We source exclusively from certified grower clusters, ensuring every carton that leaves our pack house meets international Grade A standards.

Our focus markets are Russia, the Middle East and the European Union. We handle everything from farm sourcing to port documentation — giving our global buyers a single, reliable point of contact.

Why Bananas

The World's Most
Traded Fruit

Bananas are the single most consumed fresh fruit on Earth — over 100 billion eaten every year. Rich in potassium, Vitamin B6, fibre and natural energy, they are a staple across every culture and demographic.

⚡

Natural Energy

Three natural sugars — sucrose, fructose and glucose — provide immediate and sustained energy.

❤️

Heart Health

High potassium content (422mg per fruit) supports healthy blood pressure and cardiovascular function.

🌾

Fibre Rich

Dietary fibre supports digestion and promotes a feeling of fullness — ideal for health-conscious consumers.

🧠

Vitamin B6

25% of daily Vitamin B6 in one medium banana — essential for brain development and immune function.

What We Export

Three World-Class Varieties

Each variety is sourced from specific growing regions in India, selected for export quality, shelf life and buyer preference in target markets.

⭐ Best Seller

Grand Naine Cavendish

India's most exported banana variety. Prized for its uniform size, long shelf life and sweet flavour — the preferred choice for Russian and Middle Eastern importers.

VarietyGrand Naine
Weight / finger120 – 200 g
Shelf Life18 – 22 days

Robusta

A hardy Cavendish sub-group with a rich, full flavour and thick peel — ideal for longer transit routes. Cost-effective and widely accepted across Gulf and Eastern European markets.

VarietyRobusta
Weight / finger100 – 180 g
Shelf Life14 – 18 days
Pack13 kg / carton
OriginAndhra Pradesh

Nendran

A uniquely Indian variety with thick peel, firm flesh and a distinctive flavour profile. High demand in UAE, UK and EU specialty grocery channels serving South Asian diaspora communities.
