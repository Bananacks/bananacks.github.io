About Bananacks Food Pvt Ltd

Built on India's
Most Fertile Ground

Bananacks Food Pvt Ltd is a fresh produce export company sourcing from Jalgaon, Maharashtra — the district that produces over 30% of India's total banana output. We source exclusively from certified grower clusters, ensuring every carton meets international Grade A standards.

Our focus markets are Russia, the Middle East and the EU. We manage everything from farm sourcing to port documentation — giving global buyers a single, reliable point of contact.

What We Export

Three World-Class Varieties

Each variety sourced from specific regions, selected for export quality, shelf life and buyer preference.

⭐ Best Seller

Grand Naine Cavendish

India's most-exported variety. Prized for uniform size, long shelf life and rich sweetness — the preferred choice for Russian and Middle Eastern buyers.

Weight / finger120 – 200 g
Shelf life18 – 22 days
Pack size13 – 14 kg / carton
OriginJalgaon, Maharashtra

Robusta

A hardy Cavendish sub-group with a full-bodied flavour and thick peel — ideal for longer sea transit. Widely accepted across Gulf and Eastern European markets.

Weight / finger100 – 180 g
Shelf life14 – 18 days
Pack size13 kg / carton
OriginAndhra Pradesh

Nendran

A uniquely Indian variety with thick peel and firm flesh. High demand across UAE, UK and EU specialty grocery channels serving South Asian communities.

Weight / finger200 – 300 g
Shelf life12 – 15 days
Pack size12 kg / carton
OriginKerala / Tamil Nadu
