About Bananacks

Built to Take India's
Best to the World

Bananacks Pvt Ltd is a fresh banana export company from India, specialising in premium Grade A Cavendish bananas for international importers and distributors.

We source exclusively from Jalgaon, Maharashtra — India's largest banana producing district — ensuring consistent quality, sizing, and shelf life across every shipment.
