What We Export

Our Products

Best Seller

Grand Naine Cavendish

India's premium export banana. Consistent size, excellent shelf life of 18–22 days, and the variety most preferred by Russian and Middle Eastern importers.

  • Variety Grand Naine (Cavendish)
  • Weight per finger 120–200g
  • Shelf Life 18–22 days
  • Packing 13–14 kg/carton
  • Colour at despatch Green Stage 1–2
  • Origin Jalgaon, Maharashtra

Robusta Banana

Hardy variety with a rich flavour profile, suited to longer transit routes. Ideal for buyers seeking a cost-effective alternative to Cavendish.

  • Variety Robusta
  • Weight per finger 100–180g
  • Shelf Life 14–18 days
  • Packing 13 kg/carton
  • Origin Andhra Pradesh / Tamil Nadu

Nendran Banana

A uniquely Indian variety with a distinct taste and thick peel. Growing demand in specialty markets in the UAE, UK, and EU diaspora communities.

  • Variety Nendran (Kerala)
  • Weight per finger 200–300g
  • Shelf Life 12–15 days
  • Packing 12 kg/carton
  • Origin Kerala / Tamil Nadu
Where We Sell

Our Export Markets

🇷🇺

Russia

Actively Expanding

Russia imports over $779 million in bananas annually. With Ecuador's supply disrupted by geopolitical tensions, Indian exporters have a direct and growing opportunity. We target buyers in Moscow and St. Petersburg via sea freight through Vladivostok and St. Petersburg ports.

🇦🇪

Middle East

Active Market

The UAE, Iraq, and Saudi Arabia collectively represent over 55% of India's banana export destinations. We have established trade routes and buyer relationships in this corridor, with shipments through Nhava Sheva port.

🇪🇺

European Union

Opportunity Market

EU banana production is forecast to decline significantly in 2025, pushing wholesale prices up. With India-EU FTA negotiations in advanced stages, we are building relationships with importers in the Netherlands — Europe's banana gateway.

How We Work

Farm to Your Port

01

Sourcing & Grading

We source directly from certified farmer clusters in Jalgaon (Maharashtra) and Andhra Pradesh. Every batch is graded for size, weight, and colour to ensure only Grade A produce makes the cut.

02

Pack House & Pre-Cooling

Bananas are de-handed, washed, treated, and packed in 13–14 kg export cartons at APEDA-approved pack houses. Pre-cooling to 13°C ensures fruit integrity throughout transit.

03

Documentation & Compliance

We handle all export documentation including Phytosanitary Certificate, Certificate of Origin, Packing List, Commercial Invoice, and Bill of Lading. Full compliance with destination country import regulations.

04

Reefer Container Shipping

Cargo is loaded into 40-foot reefer containers maintained at 13°C with controlled ethylene levels throughout the voyage. We follow APEDA's sea protocol for Russia shipments (18–25 day transit).

05

Delivery & After-Care

We provide full shipment tracking and share all documents digitally. We remain available for any post-delivery queries and work towards long-term repeat contracts with all buyers.
